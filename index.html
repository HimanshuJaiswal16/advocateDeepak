To file a divorce case, you must submit a petition in the Family Court within your jurisdiction. The process begins with filing a divorce petition citing valid grounds such as cruelty, desertion, mutual consent, or others under the Hindu Marriage Act or Special Marriage Act. It’s best to consult an experienced family lawyer to ensure your documentation and procedures are correct.

If you are falsely accused, it’s crucial to stay calm and immediately contact a criminal defense lawyer. Gather all possible evidence and witnesses that can support your innocence. Your advocate will help you file anticipatory bail if required and represent you throughout the proceedings.

Yes, under Section 438 of the Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC), you can apply for anticipatory bail if you apprehend arrest for a non-bailable offense. The court may grant it if satisfied that the accusation appears to be motivated or false. Advocate Deepak Tanwar provides expert assistance in filing and arguing anticipatory bail applications.

Property dispute resolution time varies depending on the case complexity, documentation, and cooperation between parties. On average, it can take anywhere from a few months to several years. However, mediation or out-of-court settlements can help achieve faster resolutions with proper legal guidance.

To draft a sale deed, you need documents such as title deed, encumbrance certificate, identity proof, property tax receipts, and a No Objection Certificate (if applicable). Advocate Deepak Tanwar ensures all legal formalities are met before registration to avoid disputes later.

A legal notice can be drafted by your advocate and sent through registered post or courier to the concerned party. It serves as a formal warning or intimation before taking legal action. Advocate Deepak Tanwar assists clients in drafting and serving legally sound notices for matters like recovery, eviction, or breach of contract.
