Family Law

Family law governs legal matters such as divorce, child custody, adoption, and domestic agreements. Our experienced attorneys guide you through sensitive issues with compassion and expertise.

Divorce & Separation

We assist clients with all aspects of divorce, including filing, settlements, asset division, spousal support, and amicable or contested proceedings.

Child Custody & Support

Our team advocates for the best interests of children, helping parents establish custody arrangements, visitation rights, and child support agreements.

Adoption & Guardianship

We guide clients through the legal process of adoption and guardianship, ensuring all requirements are met and the process is smooth and legally compliant.

Domestic Agreements & Mediation

We draft and review prenuptial, postnuptial, and separation agreements, and provide mediation services to resolve disputes amicably whenever possible.
